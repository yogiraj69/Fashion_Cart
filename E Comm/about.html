Who we are?

Fashion Cart is one of the fastest-growing e-commerce brands in India which offers a unique range of men’s street wear with a shade of western touch. We are the pioneers of bringing western style in the Indian market at very affordable rates. Founded by Amar Pawar and Raghavendra Pawar, Fashion Cart is a family-owned business that started its first retail shop in 2010. Currently, we have three retail shops in Andheri, Borivali and Thane in Mumbai. We are also successfully carrying out e-commerce operations since 2017.
